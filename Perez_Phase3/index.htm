What do you truly know about video games and a child's connection to them? Growing up, video games have always been a part of my life from brick breaker on the old BlackBerry Curve, to Elden Ring on the PC. They have been around for a long time and for a good reason. Video games are so accessible to any gender and age. No matter what you enjoy doing there is a game for you. Personally, I like first person shooters whereas my girlfriend could not care less about them. However, if you show her a game where you just have to build a bridge to get across the gap, she would be happy. This just shows how games work no matter your interests.
